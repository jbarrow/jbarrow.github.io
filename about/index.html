About

I’m Joe Barrow. This site is a working notebook for what I’m reading, building, and thinking about — mostly at the intersection of vision–language models, document understanding, and the systems that have to make sense of messy real-world inputs.

The site is split into two parts:

  • Posts — long-form pieces I’ve thought about long enough to want to put my name on. Often illustrated, often with a chart or two.
  • Field Notes — shorter, dated-loosely working notes that I revise in place. The kind of thing that would otherwise live in a personal Obsidian vault. (In fact, they do live in an Obsidian vault — see Editing in Obsidian in the docs.)

If you want to see what I’ve actually built, the software page lists tools and projects.
