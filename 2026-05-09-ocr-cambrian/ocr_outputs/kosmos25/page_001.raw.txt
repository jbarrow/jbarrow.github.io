<bbox><x_464><y_556><x_582><y_574></bbox>of the two subﬁelds.

<bbox><x_724><y_602><x_810><y_619></bbox>Representative
<bbox><x_464><y_619><x_810><y_637></bbox>work on form preparation includes the line of work on
<bbox><x_464><y_637><x_810><y_654></bbox>Form2Seq [ 1 , 2 ] and FormA11y [ 18 ]. Form2Seq formalizes
<bbox><x_464><y_654><x_810><y_671></bbox>a semantic form model, treating it as a joint classiﬁcation
<bbox><x_464><y_671><x_810><y_689></bbox>and grouping task. Lower-level elements like widgets and
<bbox><x_464><y_689><x_810><y_706></bbox>text blocks are ﬁrst classiﬁed into, e.g. ChoiceButton

<bbox><x_464><y_724><x_810><y_741></bbox>gories like ChoiceGroup . However, it is assumed that
<bbox><x_464><y_741><x_810><y_758></bbox>the interactive form widgets have already been correctly

<bbox><x_464><y_845><x_784><y_863></bbox>end-to-end preparation of a form from a ﬂat document.
