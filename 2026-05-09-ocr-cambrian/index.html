OCR's Cambrian Explosion 1 - Introduction

Strong, open VLMs enabled an explosion of open OCR model releases, with little sign of things letting up. In this survey, I detail the models, their evaluation, research trends, and open questions.

By Joe Barrow2026-05-12

Until very recently, the working list of open OCR models being used in industry could fit comfortably on a postcard. However, the release of strong, open VLMs, like the Qwen series [1,2] has led to a significant uptick in the number and accuracy of these models.
